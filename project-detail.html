Our Objectives

The main aim of (CCVP) is to restore hope for many vulnerable people by reducing human suffering through education, Livelihood, Health, Education, protection, and WASH infrastructure.

  • To renew community hope and strengthen their coping mechanism to overcome shocks and stresses caused by disasters.
  • To enhance community diversification on sustainable livelihood through integrated livelihood approaches
  • To be leaders /ambassador in child and women protection and safeguarding
  • To contribute to the realization of peace and health for sustainable human development in poor and marginalized areas.

CCVP has been working actively for past 2 years in Golbo Ward, Dabel Division while collaborating with County government of Marsabit to deliver its program effectively.

Kassim Abduba, Mentorship Director
